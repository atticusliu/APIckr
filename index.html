Welcome to APIckr!

Have you ever gone to a hackathon without any preconceived ideas? And at these hackathons, do you remain clueless as to which APIs to use as companies endlessly bombard theirs to you? Well, same. With APIckr, we've been there before. We're here to help.

What is APIckr?

APIckr is a service to help match you to an API to use. Based on a few questions we ask you, we match your interests to an API from Mashery. Our goal is to help you well on your way to building something awesome without needing to dwell on which API to use.
